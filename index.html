Antariksh Marbles curates imported and Indian marble for homes, hospitality spaces, and architectural projects that demand elegance, polish, and lasting material character.

Source To Space

Directly From The Mines

At ANTARIKSH MARBLES, we supply genuine Italian Statuario marble sourced directly from renowned quarries in Italy. Each slab reflects natural beauty and unique veining. As a result, every installation looks exclusive and refined.

In addition to our imported collection, we also operate our own marble mine in India, which allows us to maintain strict control over quality, selection, and supply.

This combination of direct quarry sourcing and our own mining operations enables us to deliver reliable materials for projects of every scale.
